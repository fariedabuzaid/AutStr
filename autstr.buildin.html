autstr.buildin.automata module¶

autstr.buildin.automata.k_longer_automaton(k, r, sigma, padding_symbol='*')¶

Creates an automaton that recognizes exactly the \((r+1)\)-tuple where the \((r+1)\)-th word is at least \(k\) letters longer than all other words in the tuple. :type k: int :param k: Minimal length distance to the other words. :type r: int :param r: Number of other words :type sigma: Set[str] :param sigma: The base alphabet :rtype: DFA :return: The k-longer-automaton
